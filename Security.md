# Security & Roadmap

Our goal is simple — make sure every techie in the trybe lands. We're building this step by step. Here's what's live and what's coming.

---

## Now

- **Fetch new projects** — daily scan of recently funded startups
- **Generate jobs** — 8 job types per project from merged data
- **Generate roadmap** — personalized career strategy, proof of work projects, and application guide with continuation prompt
- **Success probability** — check how likely you are to land a given role at a given project based on your profile and the company's stage

---

## Next

- **Post suggestions** — AI drafts posts for X, LinkedIn, and other platforms tailored to the project you're targeting
- **Cold DM drafts** — personalized outreach messages for founders, hiring managers, and team members based on your research and the project's funding context

---

## Later

- **Auto-post** — connect your X, LinkedIn, and other social accounts so the app can publish posts and DMs on your behalf
- **Approve-first mode** — same connection, but nothing goes out without your review and approval first

---

## Much Later

- **Ads assistance** — run targeted ads for your profile or projects so the right companies see you easier
- **OSINT search** — find direct contacts (emails, X handles, LinkedIn profiles) for founders, hiring managers, and decision-makers at target companies
